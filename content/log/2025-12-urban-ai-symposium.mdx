Prof. Silva presented CitySurfaces, a tool that provides researchers and city agencies with a method for collecting sidewalk material data to inform sustainability and accessibility-related policies and initiatives. He also discussed StreetAware, a project that combines audio, video, and LiDAR data from urban intersections to develop insights on accessibility and safety.
